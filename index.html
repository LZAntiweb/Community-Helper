Learn Soccer

Soccer is played by two teams of 11 players each. The main goal is to score more than the opponent. Here are some basics:

  • Positions: Forward, Midfielder, Defender, Goalkeeper.
  • Match Duration: 90 minutes, split into 2 halves.
  • Rules: No hands (except goalkeeper), offside rule, fouls, and free kicks.
  • Skills: Passing, shooting, dribbling, defending, teamwork.
